02

Why it exists

SIGNAL VS NOISE

Bungie communicates in bursts. Between those bursts, the community fills the silence with rumor, leaks, doom, and hope, and it gets hard to tell which is which. I wanted one page that separates what is actually confirmed from what is chatter, updated every day, with receipts. That page did not exist, so I built it.

03

How it works

THE HONEST VERSION

I'll be straight with you: the daily collection is automated, because no human can read every feed, subreddit, and outlet every single day. Once a day at noon Pacific, a pipeline I built and run on my own server sweeps official Bungie feeds, gaming press, and community sources, cross-references what it finds, and publishes the digest here and to Discord.

The rules it follows are mine, and they do not bend: every item must trace to a real, link-checked URL. Nothing is published without a source. Every item gets a credibility tier: OFFICIAL (straight from Bungie or a platform holder), PRESS (established outlets), STAFF (confirmed Bungie employees speaking publicly), or COMMS (community discussion, tiered honestly as unverified). Corroboration counts only independent reporting, never content farms echoing each other. Missed patch weeks get called out. Quiet days get labeled quiet instead of dressed up.

If something on this site is ever wrong, that is on me, and I want to know: reach me through my Ko-fi page.
